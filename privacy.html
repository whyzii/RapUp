Privacy Policy

RapUP · Last updated 21 June 2026

Overview

RapUP is a lyrics editor designed to be private. The app has no account, no sign-in, and requires no internet connection. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data We Collect

None. RapUP does not collect any personal information. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Your Content

All lyrics, folders, sections, and other content you create are stored only on your device. This data is never uploaded to us or to any third party, and we have no access to it. If you use your device's backup features (such as iCloud or iTunes/Finder backups), your content may be included in those backups, which are governed by Apple's own privacy terms.

Third-Party Services

RapUP does not integrate any third-party services, software development kits, or external servers.

Children's Privacy

RapUP does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
